The conference begins at 8:00 am with registration on April 23 and ends at 12:45 pm on April 24.

All talks take place in Strickland Hall. Registration will be located in Strickland Hall 213.

The poster session will be held on Saturday, April 23rd, in the Walt Disney room located in the Memorial Student Union. A dinner party in honor of Yuri Latushkin's 60th birthday will take place immediately afterward in the Stotler Lounge, also in Memorial Union. All conference participants are welcome.
