Getting to Columbia

If you are driving, Columbia is just off I-70, about 130 miles east of Kansas City and about 120 miles west of St Louis.

If you are flying, Columbia is accessible via three airports:
  • Columbia Regional Airport
    12 miles south of the University of Missouri campus.
    Note that flights are limited, so please book well in advance.

  • Lambert-St. Louis International Airport
    110 miles from Columbia. Serviced by shuttle bus:
    MoX: http://moexpress.com

  • Kansas City International Airport
    140 miles from Columbia. Serviced by shuttle bus:
    MoX: http://moexpress.com
