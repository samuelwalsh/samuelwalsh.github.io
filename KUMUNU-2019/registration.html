Registration

We ask that all attendees register by March 27th, 2019.

Click here to register online.

Poster Session

In addition to the invited and plenary talks, there will be a poster session held on Saturday before dinner. Graduate students, postdocs, and assistant professors who would like to present a poster are encouraged to apply. Please make sure that you select the YES box under the Attendance Information section of the online registration form and submit an abstract.

Logistical details will be provided closer to the date of the conference.

Travel Support

We have some limited funds available to partially support the travel for participants, especially graduate students, postdocs, and early career mathematicians. These funds will be given out on a rolling basis, with preference given to more junior mathematicains.

In order to qualify for reimbursement, make sure to check the YES box under the Attendance Information section of the online registration form. For students and postdocs, please also have your advsior or postdoc mentor send Samuel Walsh (walshsa 'at' missouri.edu) or Yuri Latushkin (latushkiny 'at' missouri.edu) a brief email explaining the relevance of the conference to your area of research. Administrative forms that must be filled out and signed, and other instructions, including how to submit receipts, will be given during the conference.
