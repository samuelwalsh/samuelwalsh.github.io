Campus Information

The conference will be held on campus in Strickland Hall, across the street from the Math Science Building.

There are several parking garages nearby on campus. The closest garage to the Strickland Hall is Turner Avenue, a five minute walk away. Parking is open and free on Saturday and Sunday. A detailed guide can be found here.

MU is a member the eduroam network. If you are visiting from another eduroam institution you can use your eduroam login on campus. Please consult your technical department to setup your account before leaving.






Around Columbia

Downtown Columbia features a number of very nice restaurants, cafes, and shops. Many of these are found along 9th Street, about a 10 minute walk north from the math department, or along Broadway Avenue. More detailed information can be found here.

There is also an extensive system of trails for hiking, running, and biking. The MKT trail can be accessed just west of the campus.

The area around MU is quite walkable, but to get to some hotels it may be necessary to drive or take a bus. Columbia has a fairly comprehensive public transportation system called COMO Connect. Please see the site for route information. There are also several local taxi companies (e.g., Taxi Terry's) as well as Uber.
